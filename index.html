How it works

Your journey to smooth, hair-free skin

Electrolysis is the only proven method of permanent hair removal — and with Angelique, every step is comfortable, clear, and personal.

01

Your Consultation

Your first visit begins with a thorough consultation to discuss the treatment options and the area to be treated. Together, you'll create a personalised plan. The consultation is complimentary if you proceed with treatment, or $20 if not — refunded at your first appointment.

02

Your Treatments

Treatments are scheduled to suit you — most clients visit weekly. After the initial clearing of the area, sessions become less frequent and shorter. Angelique's technique is as fast, effective, and painless as possible.

03

Lasting Results

Because hair grows in cycles, consistent scheduling is key to permanent results. Over time, you'll see less and less regrowth — until the treated area is permanently clear. The freedom is worth it.

FAQ

Your questions, answered

Everything you need to know before your first visit — and what to expect along the way.

Your first visit begins with a consultation. We'll go over the treatment options and the area to be treated, and create a personalised plan. The consultation is complimentary if you proceed with treatment, or $20 if not — refunded at your first appointment.

The number of treatments varies by individual, depending on hair growth cycles, hair quality, and previous removal methods. Consistent scheduling is the most important factor in achieving permanent results.

Sessions may occur every 3–4 days depending on the area and your skin's response, though most clients prefer weekly visits. After the initial clearing, treatments become less frequent and shorter in duration.

Most people find it to be relatively painless. Some experience mild discomfort or a sensation of heat, which can be managed with a topical cream beforehand, an over-the-counter pain reliever, or simply avoiding caffeine before your appointment.

Some redness and slight swelling are normal and temporary. Applying an ice pack (wrapped in a damp towel) for 15–20 seconds at a time over 10–20 minutes usually resolves this quickly. Any irritation will typically disappear within a few days.

Electrolysis has been the gold standard for over 125 years and is the only proven method of permanent hair removal. Unlike laser, which reduces growth and doesn't work for all skin and hair types, electrolysis is effective for everyone — regardless of hair or skin colour.
